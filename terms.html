2. Description of Service

Inbox Sort and Tidy is a free, client-side tool that connects to your Gmail account via the Gmail API. It scans your inbox, groups emails by sender, and lets you archive or label messages in bulk. The app runs entirely in your browser. There is no server.

3. What the App Does

  • Reads email metadata (sender, subject line, date). Never email bodies
  • Removes the INBOX label from messages (archive)
  • Adds labels to messages (Newsletters, Receipts, FYI)

4. What the App Cannot Do

  • Delete or permanently remove emails
  • Read email bodies or attachments
  • Send emails on your behalf
  • Access contacts, calendar, or any non-Gmail data
  • Store your data on any server
